What is Bobmath?

Bobmath is a high-speed portal for a wide variety of interactive, educational apps. We offer over 180 of these browser apps, dedicated to providing an easy-to-navigate, safe, and unrestricted environment for any and all enjoyers of these apps.

Our Goal

Bobmath was created with one goal in mind: entertainment. Whether you finished all of your work or feel like you need a break, simply enter bobmath into your browser and be transported into a world of high-quality apps that you can access 24/7. and most importantly, hassle-free.

Why Us?

  • One-click access: Organized GUI that allows you to find your favorite app immediately
  • 180+ Apps: Bobmath features a giant, always expanding library of apps
  • Leaderboard: Track your playtime and compete against other schools to see who is the best
  • Safe: Play directly in your browser with no downloads required
  • Fast: Fast load times means your app is ready to use in blazing-fast times

Features

Beyond games, we offer a Live School Leaderboard. Students from across the nation can register and start earning points towards their school by spending time using any app. Track the top 5 schools and compete to climb the ranks, leading your school to victory!
